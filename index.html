Set Recommendations

Guidelines based on hypertrophy research (Schoenfeld et al.):

  • Large muscles (Chest, Lats, Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes): 10-20 sets/week
  • Medium muscles (Delts, Upper Back): 8-16 sets/week
  • Small muscles (Biceps, Triceps, Calves, Forearms, Core): 6-14 sets/week

These vary by muscle group size and individual recovery capacity.

Below minimum — room to add volume
In range — optimal for growth
Above maximum — may hinder recovery
